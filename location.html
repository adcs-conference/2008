Location

ADCS2008 will be held in the auditorium at the Tasmanian ICT Centre, Hobart.


View Larger Map

The Tasmanian ICT Centre (centred in the above map) is located on the foreshore of Hobart in the centre of town overlooking the Derwent River. The coffee houses, pubs and galleries of Salamanca Place are 2-3 minutes walk.

Dinners

The conference dinner will be at Annapurna, an Indian restaurant on Salamanca Pl. This is included in your registration.

For those of you in town on Sunday night, we're having an informal dinner (means you pay yourself!) at Tacos, a Mexican restaurant on Salamanca Pl. If you would like to join us but didn't tick the box on the rego form, please drop us a line so we can be sure there's space.
